About Us

Lee's Venture is a leading manufacturing company based in Demsa Local Government, Adamawa State. We are committed to delivering exceptional manufacturing solutions that meet the highest standards of quality and precision. Our state-of-the-art facility and experienced team enable us to serve diverse industries across Nigeria.
